Understanding Price Comparisons

What does "X% cheaper" mean?

When you see a savings percentage (e.g., "40% cheaper"), it means that diaper is cheaper compared to the average price of the same brand and size at other retailers. We only show this tag when the savings is greater than 5%.

How we calculate savings

We compare a diaper's price to the average price of the same brand and size across all other retailers in our database. For example, if a Huggies Size 3 at Costco costs $0.20 per diaper while the average price elsewhere is $0.33, we'd show it as "40% cheaper".
